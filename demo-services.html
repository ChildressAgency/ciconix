As the Government consulting sector continues to evolve - we refuse to remain complacent. CICONIX is committed to offering clients a wide array of services that maximizes the expertise of our capable and proven staff.

Here are our current areas of focus and how we can assist in each area:

Program Governance

CICONIX assists in developing and maintaining the governance frameworks that enable timely and accurate decision making by program stakeholder, sponsors, and managers, especially as they may relate to the accountability and responsibilities associated with an organization's mission-critical / mission-essential capital projects. We provide highly competent professionals who are certified in Lean Six Sigma (LSS) practices and the standards encouraged by the Project Management Institute (PMI) Project Management Body of Knowledge (PMBOK) to promote structured approaches that are logical, robust and repeatable whether the goal is to streamline or enhance routine business activities or conduct transformational change initiatives to eliminate waste or reduce risk.
